About Me

As a Sustainability Data Engineer at GCX, I help organisations achieve their sustainability goals through the smart use of data and technology. I focus on processing and analysing environmental data for leading REITs and insurance companies in South Africa, as well as automating ETL pipelines with Python and SQL to improve workflows and data reliability.

I also work across the business automating workflows using tools like n8n. I assist clients in reducing their carbon footprints, setting and tracking targets, and performance-managing their portfolios. I find tackling the complexities of corporate sustainability and navigating the associated regulatory frameworks a rewarding challenge.

I enjoy leveraging tools such as Power BI, SQL, and Python to design dashboards and solutions that simplify sustainability reporting and resource management. My role involves automating and streamlining data processing to enhance data quality and deliver actionable insights to support faster, more informed decision-making.

With a first-class MSc and international experience on environmental projects, I bring a strong analytical mindset and a practical approach to solving diverse challenges.
